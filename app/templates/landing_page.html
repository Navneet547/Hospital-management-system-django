About Us

Arogyaneed Health Home is an Indian multinational healthcare group headquartered in Chennai. Along with the eponymous hospital chain, the company also operates pharmacies, primary care and diagnostic centres, telehealth clinics, and digital healthcare services among others through its subsidiaries.

The company was founded by Prathap C. Reddy in 1983 as the first corporate healthcare provider in India. Several of Arogyaneed Health Home have been among the first in India to receive international healthcare accreditation by the India-based Join admin_host Commission International (JCI)[4][5] as well as NABH accreditation.
